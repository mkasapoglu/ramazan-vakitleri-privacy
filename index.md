## English

This privacy policy describes how the 2026 Ramazan app collects, uses, stores and protects user data. The app's primary purpose is to provide prayer times and iftar/suhoor times for the selected city.

### 1. Collected Data
The app only stores the city you select and your preference in local storage; it does not collect your name, email, location or other personal data. When downloading prayer times via the internet connection, the app does not transmit device or user data.

### 2. Use of Data
The collected city information and the downloaded time data are only used to display prayer times. Data is not shared with third parties for advertising, marketing or analytics.

### 3. Data Storage and Security
Data is stored only on your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app. Since no data is stored on any server, no additional security measures are required.

### 4. Data Sharing
Since the app does not collect or store any personal data, it is not shared with third parties.

### 5. User Rights
As the app does not hold personal data, there are no deletion or correction requests. You can remove all data by deleting the app.

This privacy policy may be updated due to legal requirements or changes in the app's functionality. When the policy is updated, the current text will be shared within the app and on the web page.
